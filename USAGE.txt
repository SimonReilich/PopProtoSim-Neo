
Usage:
  proto-sim cut <x0> <t> [options]
  proto-sim mod <x0> <m> [options]
  proto-sim cmb <x0> <m> <t> [options]
  proto-sim [--help]

Options:
  -s <rt>, --sniper <rt>    Enables the sniper with rate <rt>.
  --seed <sd>               Custom seed for random number generation.
  --slow                    Prints the execution steps with delay.
  -h, --help                Show this screen.
